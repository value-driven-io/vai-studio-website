2. Information We Collect

2.1 Tourist User Data

When you register as a tourist user, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address
  • Contact Information: WhatsApp number (optional)
  • Preferences: User type (tourist/local resident), island preferences, primary language
  • Booking Information: Tour bookings, booking references, number of participants, special requirements, dietary restrictions, accessibility needs
  • Marketing Preferences: Consent for launch updates and promotional communications

2.2 Operator User Data

When you register as a tour operator, we collect:

  • Business Information: Company name, contact person, business address
  • Contact Information: Business email, WhatsApp number, phone number
  • Operational Data: Islands served, tour types offered, target booking volumes, customer preferences
  • Financial Information: Commission rates, revenue data, booking history
  • Business Credentials: Business license information, certifications
  • Languages: Languages spoken for customer communication

2.3 Booking and Transaction Data

When you make or process bookings, we collect:

  • Booking Details: Tour selections, dates, times, participant numbers
  • Pricing Information: Tour prices, discounts applied, commission calculations
  • Communication Records: Booking confirmations, status updates, customer-operator communications
  • Payment Information: Payment status (payments are processed directly between customers and operators)

2.4 Technical and Analytics Data

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, screen resolution
  • Usage Analytics: Page views, click tracking, user interactions (via Google Analytics)
  • Marketing Analytics: UTM parameters, QR code tracking, campaign attribution
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics, error logs
