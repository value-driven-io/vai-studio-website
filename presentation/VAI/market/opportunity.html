Sizing the Opportunity

From the massive global travel industry down to a specific, achievable target in French Polynesia, here's how we define the market for VAI.

Step 1: The Global Context (TAM)

The Total Addressable Market shows us the big picture. The global tourism market is worth trillions, and key trends in adventure and luxury travel perfectly align with what French Polynesia offers.

Adventure Tourism: Valued at over $400 billion, this market is growing at a remarkable 16.8% annually. These are the travelers seeking authentic, immersive experiences connected to nature and culture.

Luxury Travel: Valued at over $1.4 trillion, this segment is fueled by travelers seeking unique, customized, and private vacationsâ€”a core strength of French Polynesia's high-end offerings.

The most important trend is the move away from pre-packaged tours. Modern travelers (FITs - Free Independent Travelers) conduct their own extensive research and want to self-curate their itineraries. This creates a powerful demand for a platform that makes it easy to discover and book authentic local experiences directly.

Step 2: The Local Market (SAM)

The Serviceable Addressable Market narrows our focus to French Polynesia. It's a high-value destination where tourists stay longer and spend more, creating a rich market specifically for tours and activities.

In 2024, French Polynesia welcomed 263,766 tourists. These visitors stay for an average of 16 days and spend approximately 380,000 XPF (~$3,400) each. Based on tourism data, about 20% of this total spend is on tours and activities.

Step 3: VAI's Target Market (SOM)

The Serviceable Obtainable Market is our realistic, initial target. To calculate this, we focus on the most relevant tourist segment and assume a conservative, achievable market share.

Our primary target is not cruise ship passengers, who represent about 20% of arrivals but have very limited time on shore and book activities through the cruise line. Our focus is on the 80% of tourists (211,655) who stay on the islands. They are the independent travelers who actively research, plan, and book their own activitiesâ€”and face all the friction VAI is built to solve.

Addressable Spend (Land-Based): ~16 Billion XPF (~$144 Million USD)

We project a conservative initial market penetration of 5%. This target is based on acquiring the independent travelers who are most actively frustrated with the current clunky booking options and are seeking a better solution.
