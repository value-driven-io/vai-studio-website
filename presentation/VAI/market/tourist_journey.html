The Tourist's Journey with VAI

The VAI ecosystem is designed to intercept and improve the tourist journey at every critical stage, creating a more satisfying and memorable trip.

1. Discovery 2. Booking 3. Experience 4. Advocacy
1

Inspiration & Discovery

Finding the Hidden Gems

Current Pain Point

The discovery phase for French Polynesia is fragmented and overwhelming, making it difficult to distinguish authentic, locally-run experiences from mass-market tours, especially on less-visited islands.

VAI's Solution

The VAI Tickets PWA serves as a single, curated gateway. Its innovative "Mood-Based Matching" engine helps travelers find unique activities by desired feeling (e.g., "Adventure," "Relax"), while the "Tourism Dispersal Algorithm" actively promotes experiences in the outer archipelagos.

2

Planning & Booking

Trust and Simplicity

Current Pain Point

Booking directly with small operators can involve language barriers, insecure payments, and communication delays. Booking through large OTAs can feel impersonal and unreliable.

VAI's Solution

VAI Tickets combines convenience with local trust. It supports 8 languages, offers secure integrated payments via Stripe, and allows travelers to book multiple activities in a single transaction. The PWA's offline functionality is a key differentiator, ensuring access to tickets without a constant data connection.

3

The In-Destination Experience

Smoother & More Authentic

Current Pain Point

The quality of the tourist experience suffers when operators are stressed and use inefficient manual systems, leading to errors like double-bookings or missed pickups.

VAI's Impact

The synergy between VAI's platforms shines here. Because the local provider uses the VAI Operator app, their business is more organized. The Channel Manager eliminates double-bookings, and financial stability from 48-hour payouts reduces stress, allowing them to focus on providing a high-quality, authentic experience.

4

Post-Trip & Advocacy

Sharing and Giving Back

Current Pain Point

Many travelers feel uneasy that a large portion of their spending benefits foreign corporations rather than the local community, leaving them feeling disconnected.

VAI's Solution

VAI's "Value Retention" model provides a powerful narrative. Travelers know 89% of their payment goes directly to the operator, and the 5% Social Impact Contribution links their visit to preserving local culture and environment. This compelling story transforms them into active supporters and advocates for the destination.
