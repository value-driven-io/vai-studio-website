Tourist Journey Workflow

5 phases

Phase 1: Discovery (Pre-Trip)

  • Visit app.vai.studio (or install Web-App/PWA)
  • Select preferred language
  • Choose discovery method: Island & mood-based ("I feel adventurous") OR Browse with filters
  • View activity details (description, pricing, operator info, availability)
  • Save favorites for later consideration

Phase 2: Booking

  • Select activity and click "Book Now"
  • Choose date and available time slot
  • Enter participant count (auto price calculation)
  • Provide contact information (email, phone)
  • Select currency (XPF, USD, EUR) and pay securely
  • Receive instant confirmation with booking reference
  • Add activity to personal calendar

Phase 3: Pre-Activity Preparation

  • View booking in "Journey tab" (personal dashboard)
  • See countdown to next activity
  • Review preparation checklist
  • Message operator with special requests
  • Get directions to meeting point
  • Receive operator confirmation notification

Phase 4: During Trip

  • Access booking details offline (PWA)
  • Follow directions to meeting point
  • Communicate with operator if needed
  • Enjoy authentic local experience

Phase 5: Post-Activity

  • View completed booking in history
  • See total bookings and spending statistics
  • Leave review (planned feature)
  • Discover more activities for next visit
  • Share experiences on social media
